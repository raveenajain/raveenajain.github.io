About
Raveena (she/her) graduated from Northeastern University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics and a Minor in Animation. She's originally from New York City, but has moved to London to pursue a Master of Science in Human-Computer Interaction Design at City, University of London.

Raveena's focus and passion has always existed in the intersection of Art and Technology. She has held internships as a Computer Graphics Software Developer and as a Software Engineer/UI Designer for Web. She is currently working to expand her understanding of users and her knowledge of UX/UI Design, Product Development, and Inclusive Design. She hopes to work with new and emerging technology and to use her Animation background to make more interactive, engaging, and immersive products. Raveena's goal is to transition to a career that allows her to find creative solutions to challenging problems.
