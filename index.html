ABOUT ME ↴
Hi! I’m Raveena, a developer and designer,
who was born and raised in New York and aspires to specialize in immersive design and development. With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Mathematics (and minor in Animation) and a Master's in Human Computer Interaction Design, I love to seek out interdisciplinary work that combines art and technology and allows me to make more dynamic designs and products. While I strive to make everyday web and mobile designs as unique yet usable as possible, I also hope to work with more emerging technology. I enjoy finding creative solutions to challenging problems through working with and on interactive, engaging, immersive, and user-centered products.

Outside of work, I've taken up photography as a hobby (and took the photographs you see on this page). I enjoy wandering around whatever city I'm in with just my phone for a camera and an iced coffee. Photography has helped me look at the world around me with a new perspective.

I’m currently looking for work across the US and UK and welcome messages from potential employers. Feel free to take a look at my resume or throw me an email!
