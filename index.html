DEVELOPER AND DESIGNER
Raveena is a born and raised New Yorker who aspires to specialize in immersive design and development. With a strong foundation in and passion for both art and technology, she aims to find creative solutions to challenging problems through working with and on interactive, engaging, immersive, and user-centered products.
Raveena is currently looking for work across the US and UK and welcomes messages from potential employers.
About
Raveena (she/her) graduated from Northeastern University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics and a Minor in Animation and has just received her Master of Science in Human-Computer Interaction Design from City, University of London.

Raveena has held internships as a Computer Graphics Software Developer at a VR-based start-up and as a Software Engineer/UI Designer for a Marketing Web Application. She is currently working to expand her understanding of users and her knowledge of UX/UI Design, Product Development, and Inclusive Design. She hopes to work with new and emerging technology and to use her Animation background to make more dynamic and interactive designs and products.

Outside of work, Raveena has taken up photography as a hobby (and took the photographs you see on this page). While she loves travelling and visiting new places to take pictures, she also enjoys just wandering around the city she lives in with just her phone for a camera and an iced coffee. Photography has helped her look at the world around her from different angles and with a new perspective.
