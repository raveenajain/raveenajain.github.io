BBC's Sherlock-Theories and Metas

I've written quiet a few metas and theories about BBC's Sherlock because the writers, collectivly known to the online community as Mofftiss, have included a great deal of nuanced subtext in their work.

This page includes some of my favorite conclusions I've drawn about the show and its story and characters. If you've ever watched Sherlock, I hope you find these texts fun and interesting to read. If you've never watched Sherlock, I highly reccomend doing so!
